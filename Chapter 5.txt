Chapter 5: A Legacy of sacrifice

- Elena journeys to Kael's homeland, witnessing the desolation he sought to heal.
- She presents the artifact to his people, who revere Kael's sacrifice and vow to use the artifact wisely.
- Haunted but inspired, Elena dedicates her life to preserving tales of Kael's bravery and the lessons of the artifact.
- The treasure's legend becomes a poignant story of love, sacrifice, and the weight of choices, carried through generations to warn of the cost of salvation.
