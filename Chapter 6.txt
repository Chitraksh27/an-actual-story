Chapter 6: The Keeper's Burden

- Years later, Elena becomes a legend herself, known as the Keeper of Kael's Sacrifice.
- Despite her fame, she lives a quiet life, mapping uncharted lands and ensuring the artifact's power remains secure.
- One day, a group of adventurers arrives, seeking the artifact and claiming they can wield its power responsibly.
- Elena confronts them, telling Kael's story in vivid detail, emphasizing the cost of greed and misplaced ambition.
- Moved by her account, the adventurers abandon their quest, realizing the treasure lies in understanding, not possession.
- As they leave, Elena reflects on her journey, feeling Kael's presence in the wind, knowing his sacrifice will guide future generations.
