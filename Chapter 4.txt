Chapter 4: The Burden of Truth

- As they leave the chamber, the guardian awakes, angered by their trespass.
- Kael shields Elena, taking a near-fatal blow, and uses the artifact's power to save her, weakening himself further.
- Outside the forest, Kael confesses that he was destined to perish once he claimed the artifact.
- Elena strugges with the knowledge that she enabled his demise, but Kael reassures her that his choice was always his own.
- With Kael's dying breath, he entrusts her with delivering the artifact to his homeland.
