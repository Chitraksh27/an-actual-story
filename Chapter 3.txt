Chapter 3: The Hidden Chamber

- Elena and Kael discover the treasure's location inside a colossal underground cavern.
- The chamber reveals an artifact pulsating with energy, surrounded by murals of a tragic past - a tale of greed and sacrifice that destroyed a thriving civilization.
- Kael hesitates, revealing that his homeland's salvation requires a great price: the artifact drains the life of its bearer.
- Elena offers to carry it, but Kael insists it must be him. Their bond deepens as they argue over the burden of sacrifice.
